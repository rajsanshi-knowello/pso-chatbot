Category 7: Numbers, Dates and Time

Rules:
1. Spell out one to nine in formal body text. Use numerals for 10+.
2. Never start a sentence with a numeral. Rephrase instead.
3. Use commas in numbers with 4+ digits (e.g., 1,000; 12,500)
4. 'per cent' in body text. '%' only in tables, social media, or limited space.
5. Time: 12-hour clock format: 10:00 am / 2:30 pm (no full stops). Use 'midday' not '12 pm', 'midnight' not '12 am'.
6. Dates: '14 April 2026' format (no commas). Numeric format (dd/mm/yyyy) only in tables.

Assessment approach:
- Check for numerals 1-9 in body text (should be spelled out)
- Flag sentences starting with numerals
- Verify date format is correct (14 April 2026, not April 14, 2026)
- Check time format (10:00 am, not 10am or 10 am)
- Verify 'per cent' in body text, '%' in tables only
- Check for 12 pm / 12 am (should be midday/midnight)
- Be strict: numbers and dates are mechanical and easy to verify
