Category 4: Headings and Capitalisation

Rules:
1. Sentence case DEFAULT for all headings, subheadings, body text, captions (only first word capitalised, unless proper nouns)
2. Title case ONLY for: formal publication titles on covers, branded program names, approved presentation templates
3. Don't skip heading levels (e.g., don't jump from H1 to H3)
4. Capitalise proper nouns. Lowercase generic references. Minimal capitalisation throughout.

Assessment approach:
- Check all headings and titles for sentence case vs title case (flag title case outside the allowed exceptions)
- Verify heading hierarchy is consistent (no skipped levels)
- Check for over-capitalisation of generic terms (e.g., 'Program' should be 'program' unless it's a proper name)
- Assess whether capital letters are used only for proper nouns
- Be strict: capitalisation rules are mechanical and easy to fix
