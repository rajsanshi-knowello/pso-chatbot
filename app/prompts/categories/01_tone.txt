Category 1: Tone of Voice

Rules:
1. PSO tone is professional, approachable and evidence-based — objective, impartial and easy to understand.
2. Flag promotional language: excited, proud, thrilled, leading, game-changing, transforming, cutting-edge
3. Flag overstatement: claims must be proportionate and evidence-based
4. Flag vague stakeholder attribution: 'Stakeholders strongly support' needs specifics
5. Formal reports/briefs use third person, neutral. Presentations/social allow first person.
6. Light advocacy only in thought leadership, strategic positioning, policy submissions — never in standard reports

Assessment approach:
- Skim for promotional words and overstatements
- Check for vague attributions without specifics
- Assess formality level (is tone appropriate for document type?)
- Flag claims that lack evidence or seem exaggerated
- Be proportionate: one enthusiastic phrase is minor; pervasive promotional language is a finding
